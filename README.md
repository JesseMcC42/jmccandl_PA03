# jmccandl_PA03
 cs425 Final Project Cat Game

This project is a game where you play as a cat. Your job it to collect all your cat toys before loosing your nine lives. You lose lives by getting hit by the dogs or the human. Both will restart you to the begining of the level, but coming into contact with dogs is scary! So you will panic and loose all your toys as well. 

Control with arrow keys. q to quit.
